Dimitri Hoffmann

Manager

Dimitri Hoffmann graduated from the Conservatoire de Musique de Genève and from the Academy of Music and Theatre Hamburg, hebcombined the artistic, educational and organizational activities in his student years. As a member of well-known European orchestras such as the Orchestre de la Suisse Romande, the NDR Symphony Orchestra Hamburg, the DSO Berlin, the Hamburg State Opera, the Capitole de Toulouse and Palau de les Arts Reina Sofia de Valencia, Dimitri worked with legendary conductors of our times such as Lorin Maazel, Zubin Mehta, Riccardo Chailly, Herbert Blomstet. As a chamber musician he has worked with Jose Carreras and Natalia Gutman, the German Scardanelli Quartet and the Israeli-Swiss Aviv Quartet. He collected his first teaching experience at the Brahms Conservatory in Hamburg.

In 2005 he founded the agency Alma Musica, which specializes in musical education in Europe, where the graduates from around the world can find teachers from European conservatoires and faculty can select perspective musicians.

Since 2013 the agency has recorded traditional artist management activities and represents world-known orchestras, conductors, chamber music ensembles and soloists.
